Volunteer




1. Fill out the volunteer application form

2. Attend a brief training session

3. Sign up for a volunteer shift at our SignUp.com website. You will not be required to create an account to sign up!



All new volunteers must sign a waiver form before your first food rescue. You will receive the waiver form once your application is submitted. If you have any inquiries, send us an email at volunteerlondon@reharvest.ca.

Other volunteer opportunities for special events and new initiatives will be posted on our Facebook page. Like and follow us to learn about new opportunities to get involved!
